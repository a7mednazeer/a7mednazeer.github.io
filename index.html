About Me

Hello, I’m Ahmed Nazeer, a Software Engineer and Computer Science graduate from Misr University for Science and Technology (MUST), with a strong academic record and hands-on experience in software engineering, Flutter mobile development, back-end systems, and AI-powered solutions. I graduated with a CGPA of 3.89 and earned an Excellent grade on my graduation project, reflecting both my academic dedication and passion for building impactful technology.

During my academic journey, I focused heavily on problem-solving and competitive programming, participating in the Egyptian Collegiate Programming Contest (ECPC), which significantly strengthened my analytical thinking, algorithmic problem-solving, and logical reasoning skills. This foundation played a major role in shaping my approach to software development and engineering challenges.

I have practical experience in software development as a Back-End Developer, working with PHP and Laravel to build scalable, reliable, and maintainable systems. Alongside that, I developed multiple cross-platform Flutter applications with clean architecture, efficient state management, modern UI/UX, and seamless API integration, with a strong focus on performance and usability.

I also worked as a Flutter Instructor at Step By Step (SBS) Student Activity, where I trained students in Dart, Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), Flutter fundamentals, and real-world mobile application development. This experience strengthened my communication, leadership, mentoring, and technical explanation skills.

My graduation project, “Recyclha – Your AI-Powered Recycling Companion,” is a smart recycling system that combines mobile development, embedded systems, and artificial intelligence to automate waste sorting. The system integrates deep learning models for material and brand recognition, deployed on Raspberry Pi, with Arduino-based hardware control for intelligent real-world automation.

In addition, I authored and published a research paper on deep learning-based waste processing and brand recognition at a Springer-affiliated international conference (AMLTA’25), reflecting my strong interest in applied AI research and intelligent systems.

I am deeply passionate about artificial intelligence and machine learning, particularly in building practical, real-world solutions that bridge mobile development, back-end engineering, and AI to create scalable, impactful, and production-ready technology.
